General purpose adjustable bipolar DC voltage supply

Delivers currents up to 250mA

The power supply is a general-purpose adjustable bipolar (+/-) DC voltage supply, designed to be very stable and spectrally pure while delivering variable amounts of current (up to about 250 mA). It utilizes 3-terminal linear voltage regulators and basic half-wave rectifiers. It also incorporates two potentiometers that allow the user the ability to adjust the negative and positive voltage rails.
